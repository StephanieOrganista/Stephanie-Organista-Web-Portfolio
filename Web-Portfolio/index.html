Who I Am

Computer Science Student at The University of Houston

Hi, my name is Stephanie Organista. I am pursuing a bachelor’s degree in computer science at the University of Houston. Some organizations that I have been involved in include the Society of Hispanic Engineers (SHEP), Cougar CS, and CS Girls and I have assumed a leadership role through my work with Phi Theta Kappa, an honor society, as vice-president and through the Student Government Association as a Parliamentarian.

My passions have always revolved around technology, and it’s because of this that my experiences are tailored towards programming, this is where I was able to develop and integrate crucial skills such as: analytical thinking, creative problem solving, and the variety of coding languages I have learned. I have taken part in innovative projects, and given back to the community by working at a local charity.
